Start with the outcome

Choose the tool based on the decision, conversion, format, or check you need to complete. The best result comes from clear inputs and a quick review of edge cases.

Recommended tools

  • Date Duration Calculator: Calculate days, weeks, months, and years between two dates.
  • Date Add or Subtract Calculator: Add or subtract days, weeks, months, and years from a date.
  • Business Days Calculator: Count weekdays between two dates and optionally exclude common weekend days.
  • Age Calculator: Calculate age in years, months, days, and total days.
  • Countdown Calculator: Calculate time remaining until a target date and time.

Review the result

Browser-based tools are fast, but input quality still matters. Check assumptions, units, formats, and context before using results in important work.
